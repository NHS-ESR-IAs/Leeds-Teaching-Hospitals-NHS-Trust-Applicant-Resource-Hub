Day One

All new substantive staff members must attend Corporate Induction on the first day of appointment. Your new line manager will arrange this for you and you will be sent the details via email closer to your start date. Temporary staff such as Bank, Agency, Locum and those on Honorary contracts may attend at their line manager’s discretion.

Corporate Induction is an all-day session and takes place on the first working day within the Trust. By the end of the Corporate Induction process the majority of all Mandatory Training will have been completed, therefore most staff will arrive in the workplace with their Mandatory Training requirements satisfied.

A Local Induction should be completed within 28 days of commencing work within the Trust. Local Induction is mandatory for all staff including temporary, Bank, Agency, Locum and those on Honorary contracts. It underpins the overall knowledge gained during the Corporate Induction process. Local Induction introduces new staff to local arrangements and identifies outstanding Mandatory Training, specifically Equality & Diversity and resuscitation training, among others.
